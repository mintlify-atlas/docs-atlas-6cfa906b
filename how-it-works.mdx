Car Bot handles the entire early-stage sales process automatically, from the moment a customer lands on your dealership site to the point where a qualified buyer is handed off to your team. This page walks through that flow step by step and covers everything the AI can do on your behalf.

## The automation flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer visits your dealership site">
    A potential buyer opens your dealership website. The Car Bot widget — a red floating chat bubble — appears in the bottom right corner of every page. It is available immediately, with no page load delay, on any device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customer starts a conversation">
    The buyer clicks the chat bubble and types a question. For example: *"Is the Toyota Corolla 2020 still available?"* or *"What's the price range for your used SUVs?"*

    Car Bot receives the message instantly and begins processing it against your live inventory catalog.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Car Bot responds with accurate inventory details">
    Car Bot queries your vehicle catalog in real time and replies with precise information — availability, pricing, condition, photos, and any relevant specs — pulled directly from the vehicle's detail page.

    The response is delivered in seconds, at any hour, without any staff involvement.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Lead qualification">
    Car Bot asks a short series of qualifying questions to assess the buyer's intent and readiness:

    - What is your budget range?
    - Are you looking to buy in the near term or just browsing?
    - Are you interested in financing options?

    Based on the responses, Car Bot scores the lead and categorizes the buyer. High-intent buyers move forward in the flow; early-stage browsers are kept engaged with follow-up content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Appointment scheduling">
    If the buyer expresses strong interest, Car Bot offers available test drive time slots synced directly with your dealership calendar. The buyer selects a slot, and the appointment is confirmed automatically — no phone tag, no manual coordination.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Handoff to a human agent">
    When a buyer asks something Car Bot cannot answer with confidence — a complex financing scenario, a trade-in appraisal, or a highly specific vehicle request — the bot transfers the conversation to a live agent.

    Your team is notified immediately via WhatsApp or email, with the full conversation history included so there is no need to re-ask questions the buyer already answered.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Analytics captured">
    Every interaction — messages sent, questions asked, qualification scores, appointments booked, and handoffs triggered — is recorded and fed into your real-time analytics dashboard. You see the complete picture of your sales funnel in one place.
  </Step>
</Steps>
