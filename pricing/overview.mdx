Car Bot offers three plans designed to fit dealerships at every stage of growth. Whether you run a small lot with a handful of vehicles or a large multi-brand concession, there is a plan sized for your operation. All plans are billed monthly with no long-term commitment required, and every new account starts with a 14-day free trial.

<Note>
  Every plan includes a **14-day free trial** with no credit card required. You can explore the full feature set of your chosen plan before you are charged anything.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Most growing dealerships get the best return from the **Premium** plan. It includes WhatsApp integration, advanced AI negotiation, and full analytics — the tools that drive the highest conversion rates on the platform.
</Tip>
