## How to get help

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check the FAQ">
    Most common questions are already answered at [/faq](/faq). Start there to get an instant answer without waiting for a reply.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Email sales for plan questions">
    Send your question to [ventas@carbot.ai](mailto:ventas@carbot.ai) if you need help choosing a plan, want to see a demo, or are ready to get started.

  <Step title="Email support for technical issues">

  <Step title="Call for urgent matters">

  </Step>
</Steps>
