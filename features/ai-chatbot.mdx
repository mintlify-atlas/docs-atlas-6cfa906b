Car Bot's AI chatbot is a floating widget that sits in the bottom-right corner of every page on your dealership site. It greets every visitor the moment they arrive, answers questions about any vehicle in your catalog, sends photos and videos on request, and can negotiate pricing within the rules you define — all without any human involvement. When a conversation goes beyond what the bot can handle, it transfers seamlessly to a human agent and sends a notification via WhatsApp or email.

## Where the chatbot appears

The widget is always visible in the bottom-right corner of every page on your dealership site. It uses a red bubble icon consistent with your brand color. Visitors can open it at any point in their browsing session without navigating away from the page they are viewing.

## How to configure the chatbot personality

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard settings">
    Log in to your Car Bot dashboard and navigate to **Settings** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a tone">
    Select one of three personality presets: **Formal** for professional, corporate-style communication; **Friendly** for a conversational and approachable feel; or **Sales-focused** for a direct, conversion-oriented style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your inventory">
    Link your vehicle catalog so Car Bot has accurate, up-to-date information about every car you have in stock. The bot uses this data to answer buyer questions precisely.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test using the floating widget">
    Open any page on your site and click the red chat bubble to interact with the bot directly. Adjust settings and test again until the responses match your expectations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Handoff to human agents

When a customer's question falls outside the bot's configured scope — for example, a request for a custom financing arrangement — Car Bot flags the conversation, notifies your team, and keeps the customer informed that a human will follow up shortly. This handoff process happens in real time and does not require the customer to start a new conversation.

For more on how leads are scored and routed after a conversation, see [Lead qualification](/features/lead-qualification).
