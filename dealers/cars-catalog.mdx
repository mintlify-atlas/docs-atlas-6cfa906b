The Car Bot vehicle catalog is a searchable inventory of every vehicle listed by dealerships in the network. Buyers can filter by brand, price range, and condition to find the right match. Behind the scenes, Car Bot queries this same catalog during conversations — so when a buyer asks about a specific model, the bot pulls live, accurate details directly from your listings.

## Searching and filtering

To browse the catalog, navigate to the **Cars** section of the platform. The following filters are available:

- **Text search**: type any make or model name to narrow results instantly
- **Brand dropdown**: filter by manufacturer — All, Toyota, Honda, Jeep, and more as dealers add inventory
- **Max price slider**: drag to set a ceiling up to $100,000; results update in real time
- **Condition toggle**: switch between **New** and **Used** to show only the vehicles that match

## What each vehicle card shows

Every listing in the catalog displays:

- Make, model, and year
- Price (USD)
- Mileage
- Fuel type (gasoline or diesel)
- Location (city)
- Condition badge: **New** or **Used**

## How Car Bot uses your catalog

When a buyer starts a conversation with your Car Bot chatbot, the bot queries your inventory in real time to answer questions about price, availability, mileage, and features. If a buyer asks "Do you have any diesel SUVs under $40,000?", the bot checks your catalog and responds with matching vehicles — including photos if available.

<Note>
  Car Bot answers buyer questions using real-time data from your inventory. Keeping your catalog up to date leads to more accurate bot responses and better buyer experiences. Stale listings can result in the bot quoting prices or availability that no longer reflect your stock.
</Note>
