The Car Bot dealer network is a curated directory of dealerships that use Car Bot to automate their sales process. Every listing is verified, and buyers can search the network by name or location in the Dealers section of the platform to find a dealership near them. Being listed in the network increases your visibility and signals to buyers that your dealership uses a modern, AI-powered experience.

## Dealer types

There are two categories of dealers in the network:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Official dealers" icon="badge-check">
    Certified directly by Car Bot. Official dealers meet the highest quality standards, maintain top ratings, and have full access to all Car Bot features — including WhatsApp integration, calendar sync, and advanced analytics.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Independent dealers" icon="handshake">
    Verified partners who have completed the Car Bot onboarding process. Independent dealers have access to core Car Bot features and appear in the public dealer directory alongside Official partners.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to find a dealer

Visit the Dealers section of the platform to browse the full network. Use the search bar to filter by dealership name or city. Each dealer profile shows their inventory count, rating, location, and certification type so you can quickly identify the right partner.

## Benefits of joining the network

When your dealership appears in the Car Bot network directory, you get:

- **Certified badge** displayed on your profile, visible to all buyers searching the network
  - **Increased visibility** — buyers browsing the Car Bot dealer directory can discover your inventory without visiting your site directly
- **Access to Car Bot tools** — manage your chatbot, inventory, leads, and appointment calendar from one dashboard
- **Priority placement** on Official plans based on rating and activity

## How to join the Car Bot dealer network

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register your account">
    Create your dealership account using the registration form on the Car Bot platform. You'll need your dealership name, contact email, and location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your inventory and configure your chatbot">
    Upload your vehicle catalog through the dashboard. Car Bot will immediately use your inventory to answer buyer questions. Customize your chatbot's name, greeting, and response style to match your brand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a qualifying plan">
    Premium and Enterprise plans are recommended for full network visibility, Official certification eligibility, and access to features like WhatsApp integration and calendar sync. See [Plans & Pricing](/pricing/overview) for a full comparison.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live in the directory">
    Once your profile is complete and your plan is active, your dealership becomes searchable in the public dealer directory. Buyers can find you by name, city, or vehicle type.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Official certification is reviewed by the Car Bot team after your account has been active for at least 30 days and meets minimum rating and activity thresholds.
</Note>
