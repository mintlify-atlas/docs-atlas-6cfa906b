The Car Bot analytics dashboard gives you a live view of how your AI sales assistant is performing. At a glance you can see how many conversations the bot has handled, how many leads it has qualified, and how much estimated revenue those interactions represent — all without digging through spreadsheets or waiting for end-of-day reports.

## Bot activity chart

The bot activity chart is a bar graph that plots the number of conversations by day of the week, Monday through Sunday. It helps you identify when your dealership receives the most customer interest so you can ensure your team is ready to handle warm handoffs on your busiest days. In the demo data, activity peaks on Saturday — the most common day for car shopping.

## Understanding your traffic sources

The traffic sources pie chart breaks down where your visitors are coming from. Each segment is color-coded so you can identify your strongest acquisition channels at a glance.

| Source | Conversations | Color |
|--------|--------------|-------|
| Web Direct | 400 | Red (#E10600) |
| Facebook | 300 | Black |
| Instagram | 300 | Gray |
| Referrals | 200 | Light gray |

Web Direct represents visitors who navigate to your site without a social media referral — typically returning customers or people who found you through search. Facebook and Instagram together account for 600 conversations in the demo, making social media your largest combined source. Referrals from other sites or partner dealerships contribute 200 additional conversations.

Use this breakdown to decide where to invest your marketing budget. If Instagram is driving high-quality leads relative to its volume, it may warrant more spend.

## Recent activity log

The activity log on the right side of the dashboard records timestamped events as they happen:

- **Appointment scheduled** — a test drive was booked through the bot
- **Lead qualified** — a visitor was scored as high-intent
- **Price sent** — Car Bot shared a price or quote with a buyer
- **New inquiry** — a fresh conversation was started

Each entry appears in real time, giving you and your team a live feed of what the bot is doing throughout the day. Clicking an entry shows you more detail about the specific conversation.
