This page answers the questions dealerships ask most often before and after getting started with Car Bot. If your question is not covered here, reach out to the team directly.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does Car Bot integrate with my inventory?">
    Car Bot connects directly to your CRM or current website via API or secure scraping. If you use Excel, we can also import your inventory from there automatically.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can the bot schedule real appointments?">
    Yes. Car Bot syncs with your Google Calendar or Outlook. When a customer wants a test drive, the bot checks your availability and schedules the appointment without any intervention.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if the bot doesn't know the answer?">
    If the bot encounters a very specific question it can't answer, it immediately transfers the conversation to a human agent and notifies you by WhatsApp or email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I customize the bot's tone?">
    Absolutely. You can configure whether you want it to be formal, friendly, or focused on aggressive sales. It also learns from your best salespeople.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a long-term contract?">
    No. Our plans are monthly and you can cancel at any time without penalties.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I try Car Bot before paying?">
    Yes, Car Bot offers a 14-day free trial on all plans. No credit card is required to start. You can explore the full feature set of your chosen tier before you are charged anything.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What channels does Car Bot work on?">
    Car Bot works via the web chat widget on your dealership site. Premium and Enterprise plans also include WhatsApp integration, letting you reach buyers on the channel they already use every day.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I access my analytics dashboard?">
    After logging in, click **Dashboard** in the sidebar. You will see KPI cards for total conversations, active inventory, qualified leads, and estimated sales value — all updated in real time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I upgrade my plan?">
    Go to your account settings in the Dashboard and select a new plan under **Billing**. Changes take effect immediately and your billing date resets to the day of the upgrade.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
