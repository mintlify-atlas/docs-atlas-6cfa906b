Car Bot is an AI-powered sales automation platform built for automotive dealerships of every size. This page explains what Car Bot does, the core problem it solves, and where to go next to get your dealership up and running.

## The problem Car Bot solves

Dealerships lose leads every day — not because the cars aren't right, but because no one is available to respond at the right moment. A buyer browses your inventory at 10 PM, has a question about a specific model, and moves on when they don't get an answer. Car Bot eliminates that gap by handling every customer conversation instantly, around the clock, with no human intervention required.

<Note>
Car Bot does not replace your sales team. It handles the repetitive, time-sensitive work so your team can focus on the conversations that close deals.
</Note>

## What Car Bot does

Car Bot combines four capabilities into a single platform:

- **AI chatbot** — A floating chat widget available on every page of your dealership site. It answers questions about specific vehicles, pricing, availability, and financing options in real time.
- **Lead qualification** — Car Bot asks qualifying questions to assess purchase intent and budget, then surfaces high-intent buyers to your team. Browsers stay in the funnel; serious buyers get escalated.
- **Inventory integration** — Your full vehicle catalog — searchable by make, model, price, and condition (new or used) — is connected directly to the AI. Answers are always based on your live inventory.
- **Analytics dashboard** — Every conversation, lead, and scheduled appointment feeds a real-time dashboard. You see conversation volume, qualified lead counts, traffic sources, and estimated sales metrics in one place.

## Who Car Bot is for

Car Bot serves automotive dealerships at every scale:

- **Independent dealers** — Starter plan supports up to 20 vehicles with full chatbot and lead qualification.
- **Mid-size dealerships** — Premium plan covers up to 100 vehicles and adds WhatsApp integration for follow-ups.
- **Enterprise networks** — Custom plans support unlimited inventory and multi-dealer network management.
