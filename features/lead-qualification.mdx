Most dealership traffic is made up of people who are curious, not committed. Salespeople spend hours answering questions for visitors who are months away from a purchase — time that could go toward closing deals with buyers who are ready today. Car Bot solves this by qualifying every lead during the conversation itself, so your team receives only prospects who are worth their attention.

## How lead qualification works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visitor opens chat and asks about a car">
    A visitor clicks the red chat bubble and asks about a vehicle — price, availability, features, or financing. Car Bot responds immediately with accurate information from your inventory.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Car Bot engages and asks qualifying questions">
    During the natural flow of the conversation, Car Bot gathers key details: the buyer's budget range, how soon they plan to purchase, and whether they have a specific model in mind. These questions feel conversational, not like a form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Bot scores the lead based on responses">
    Car Bot evaluates the answers against your configured scoring criteria. A buyer with a defined budget, a short purchase timeline, and interest in a specific vehicle scores higher than someone who is broadly exploring options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="High-intent leads are flagged and your team is notified">
    When a lead crosses your threshold score, Car Bot marks it as high-intent, logs it in your dashboard activity feed, and sends a real-time notification to your sales team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Low-intent leads receive automated nurture follow-ups">
    Visitors who are not yet ready to buy are placed into an automated follow-up sequence. Car Bot re-engages them at intervals you configure, keeping your dealership top of mind until they are ready to move forward.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard metrics

The **Qualified Leads** card on your dashboard tracks high-intent buyers identified by Car Bot. In the platform demo, this number stands at 86 — a 24% increase over the prior period. This metric updates in real time as conversations happen throughout the day.

You can view individual lead details in the recent activity log, which timestamps every qualification event and includes a brief summary of the buyer's interest. Combined with the bot activity chart, you can see which days and traffic sources generate the highest-quality leads.
