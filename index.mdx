## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your dealer account">
    Sign up at the Car Bot registration page. Enter your dealership name, email, and a secure password to get started.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your vehicle inventory">
    Upload your inventory via the dashboard. Car Bot learns your catalog so it can answer buyer questions with accurate details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate the AI chatbot">
    Enable the floating chat widget on your dealership site. Car Bot will immediately begin handling customer inquiries and qualifying leads.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor your dashboard">
    Visit the analytics dashboard to track conversation volume, lead quality scores, appointment bookings, and estimated sales revenue.
  </Step>
</Steps>
