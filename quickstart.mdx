---
title: "Get started with Car Bot: dealer onboarding guide"
description: "Set up your Car Bot dealer account, add your vehicle inventory, activate the AI chatbot, and start qualifying leads — all in under 30 minutes."
---

Car Bot gets your dealership running with AI-powered sales automation in a handful of steps. This guide walks you through account creation, inventory setup, chatbot activation, and your first look at the analytics dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to the Car Bot registration page. Fill in your first name, last name, dealership name, country, estimated inventory size, and corporate email address. Choose a password, then click **Probar Demo Gratis** to create your account.

    <Info>
      Your account is tied to your corporate email. After registering, you are redirected automatically to the dashboard where you can start setting up your dealership.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your dealer profile">
    After logging in, you are taken directly to the dashboard. From here, configure your dealership name and any settings relevant to your operation.

    Your dashboard is the control center for everything Car Bot tracks — leads, conversations, inventory counts, and scheduled appointments.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your vehicle inventory">
    Navigate to the vehicle catalog page to access your inventory. Car Bot supports browsable inventory with the following filters:

    - **Search** — find vehicles by keyword
    - **Price range** — drag a slider to set a maximum price ceiling up to $100,000
    - **Condition** — filter by New or Used
    - **Make** — narrow results to a specific manufacturer

    Each vehicle has its own detail page with photos, specs, and pricing. The AI chatbot reads from this catalog in real time to answer customer questions accurately.

    <Note>
      The number of vehicles you can list depends on your plan: up to 20 on Starter, up to 100 on Premium, and unlimited on Enterprise. See [plans and pricing](/pricing/overview) for details.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate the AI chatbot">
    The Car Bot widget is available automatically on every page of your dealership site — you do not need to install or embed any code manually.

    Look for the red chat bubble in the bottom right corner of any page. Click it to open the chat interface and interact with a live demo of the bot's capabilities.

    In your dashboard settings, you can configure the bot's personality:

    - **Formal** — professional tone suited to premium or luxury inventory
    - **Friendly** — conversational tone for a welcoming buyer experience
    - **Sales-focused** — direct language optimized for conversion

    <Tip>
      Use the floating widget on the Car Bot homepage to test different conversation flows before going live with customers.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor leads and analytics">
    Return to your dashboard at any time to view real-time performance metrics:

    | Metric | What it shows |
    |--------|--------------|
    | Conversations | Total chatbot sessions started |
    | Leads qualified | Buyers who passed qualification criteria |
    | Inventory count | Number of active vehicles in your catalog |
    | Estimated sales | Projected revenue from qualified leads |

    The dashboard also includes a **bot activity chart** showing conversations per day, and a **traffic sources breakdown** across Web, Facebook, Instagram, and Referrals.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Check>
Your dealership is ready. Car Bot is live, your inventory is connected, and leads are being qualified automatically. Head to your dashboard to watch it work.
</Check>
