Car Bot's inventory management connects your vehicle catalog directly to the AI chatbot. When you add a car to your inventory, the bot immediately learns its details — make, model, year, price, mileage, fuel type, location, and condition — and can discuss it accurately with any customer who asks. The result is a chatbot that always has the right answer, without you having to script anything manually.

## The cars catalog page

Your inventory is available to buyers through the cars catalog at `/cars`. It presents your vehicles in a searchable, filterable grid that works on any device. Buyers can narrow down the list to find exactly what they are looking for before or during a conversation with Car Bot.

### Available filters

| Filter | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Search | Free-text search by make or model name |
| Brand | Dropdown to filter by manufacturer |
| Max price | Slider from $0 to $100,000 |
| Condition | Toggle between New and Used vehicles |

## Vehicle data shown to buyers

Each vehicle card in the catalog displays the following details:

- Make and model
- Year
- Price
- Mileage
- Fuel type (gasoline, diesel, hybrid, electric)
- Location
- Condition (New or Used)

Car Bot uses this same data during conversations. When a buyer asks "what's the mileage on the Hilux?" or "do you have any new SUVs under $35,000?", the bot queries your live inventory to give an accurate answer.

## Adding vehicles to your inventory

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your dashboard">
    Log in to your Car Bot account and open the main dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to the inventory section">
    Select **Inventory** from the sidebar to open your vehicle management panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add vehicle details">
    Enter the following for each new vehicle: make, model, year, price, mileage, fuel type, location, and condition. All fields are used by the bot during customer conversations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Car Bot learns the vehicle automatically">
    Once you save the entry, Car Bot incorporates the new vehicle into its knowledge base. Customers can ask about it immediately — no additional configuration required.
  </Step>
</Steps>
