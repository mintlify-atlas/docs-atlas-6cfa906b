Car Bot handles test drive scheduling end-to-end, from the moment a buyer expresses interest through to the confirmed calendar entry — all without your team lifting a finger. When a customer asks about a vehicle during a chat, the bot shows photos, shares pricing, and proactively offers available time slots. Once the customer picks a time, the appointment is written directly to your calendar.

## Scheduling flow example

The following exchange is taken from a real Car Bot conversation demo:

```text
Customer: Yes, please. Is the price negotiable?

Car Bot:  Here are some photos of the interior and exterior. The list price 
          is $22,500 USD. We can schedule a visit so you can test it and 
          discuss numbers. Does tomorrow at 10 AM work for you?
```

The bot handles the transition from product question to appointment offer naturally, without requiring a human agent to step in.

## How appointment scheduling works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Customer asks about a vehicle during chat">
    The buyer starts a conversation through the Car Bot widget on your site or WhatsApp channel. They ask about a specific vehicle — make, model, price, or availability.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Car Bot shows photos, pricing, and details">
    The bot retrieves live data from your inventory catalog and responds with vehicle details, condition, mileage, fuel type, and photos or videos if available.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer shows interest — bot proposes test drive slots">
    When the buyer signals interest (asking about pricing flexibility, next steps, or visiting the dealership), Car Bot checks your calendar availability and offers one or more concrete time slots in the conversation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customer confirms a time">
    The buyer selects a slot directly in the chat. No forms, no callbacks, no waiting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Car Bot adds the appointment to your calendar and notifies you">
    The confirmed appointment is written to your connected Google Calendar or Outlook account. You receive a notification so you're ready when the customer arrives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic reminders sent to reduce no-shows">
    On Premium and Enterprise plans, Car Bot sends automatic reminders to the customer before the appointment — reducing no-shows without any manual follow-up from your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Calendar integrations

Car Bot syncs confirmed appointments with your existing calendar system. Supported integrations:

- **Google Calendar** — appointment appears instantly in your connected Google account
- **Microsoft Outlook** — syncs with your Outlook calendar via Microsoft 365

<Note>
  Calendar sync is available on Premium and Enterprise plans. Starter plan users receive appointment notifications by email but do not get direct calendar integration. See [Plans & Pricing](/pricing/overview) to compare plans.
</Note>

## What happens after a confirmed booking

Once the customer confirms a time slot:

1. The appointment is added to your dealership calendar
2. You receive a notification (WhatsApp or email, depending on your plan)
3. The customer receives a confirmation message through the same chat channel
4. Reminder messages go out automatically before the appointment time (Premium/Enterprise)

## When the bot can't answer a scheduling question

If Car Bot encounters a scheduling scenario it can't handle — such as a complex rescheduling request or a customer with specific requirements outside normal slots — it transfers the conversation to a human agent and sends you a notification via WhatsApp or email so you can follow up directly.

## Automatic follow-up with undecided leads

<Tip>
  Car Bot automatically follows up with leads who showed interest but didn't schedule an appointment. The bot re-engages undecided buyers with a friendly message, keeping your pipeline warm without any manual outreach from your team.
</Tip>
